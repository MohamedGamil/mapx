## Scope

Implements changes to remove false edges and noise from graph traversal features.

1. **F37 — Graph Resolution & Noise Reduction** (🟠 HIGH):
   - Excludes built-in JS/TS globals (`Date`, `Error`, `Map`, `Set`, `Promise`, etc.) from matching user-defined symbols of the same name.
   - Filters out import-level `<top-level>` reference entries from the call graph.
   - Restructures `mapx_impact` risk grading into HIGH, MEDIUM, and LOW risk based on calling depth (depth 1 is HIGH, depth 2+ is MEDIUM/LOW) and try/catch or test file status.
   - Implements disk checks to output a filesystem staleness warning header when modifications occur without scanning.
